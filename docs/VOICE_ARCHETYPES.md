# Voice Archetypes for TDA NPCs

To ensure a rich and varied tavern experience while keeping voice production manageable, the 37 NPCs are grouped into **9 Voice Archetypes**. These archetypes serve as the foundation for future voice-over (.wav) recording.

## 1. The Sage (Wise & Elderly)
*   **Tone:** Calm, measured, philosophical, slightly gravelly.
*   **Pacing:** Slow and deliberate.
*   **NPCs:** Obaya, Sister Elena, Laeral Silverhand.

## 2. The Warrior (Gruff & Direct)
*   **Tone:** Low-pitched, gritty, authoritative, no-nonsense.
*   **Pacing:** Steady, clipping consonants.
*   **NPCs:** Audhild, Captain Zylos, Keth, Bruenor, Raiding Talon, Hadrian.

## 3. The Noble (Refined & Arrogant)
*   **Tone:** Formal, posh, condescending but elegant.
*   **Pacing:** Flowing, slightly enunciated.
*   **NPCs:** Lady Alustriel, Matron Malice, Sssilas, Lord Elrond.

## 4. The Scoundrel (Cunning & Sly)
*   **Tone:** Whispering, fast-talking, street-smart, raspy.
*   **Pacing:** Erratic, quick bursts.
*   **NPCs:** Viconia, Tana, Jarlaxle.

## 5. The Worker (Hardy & Friendly)
*   **Tone:** Boisterous, warm, thick regional accents, earthy.
*   **Pacing:** Energetic, rhythmic.
*   **NPCs:** Elowen, Hilda, Donaar, Thrak, Tordek, Barnaby.

## 6. The Host (Jovial & Welcoming)
*   **Tone:** Deep, hearty, resonant, inviting.
*   **Pacing:** Smooth, theatrical.
*   **NPCs:** Berrinar, Grog, Durnan, Mirt.

## 7. The Stalker (Watchful & Quiet)
*   **Tone:** Breathy, soft, observant, almost monotone.
*   **Pacing:** Very slow, pauses between sentences.
*   **NPCs:** Hoot, Slow-Shell, Irilia.

## 8. The Adept (Focused & Studious)
*   **Tone:** Analytical, energetic, technical, high-pitched.
*   **Pacing:** Fast, overlapping thoughts.
*   **NPCs:** Aris, Kava, Morgana, Adrik.

## 9. The Wild (Unpredictable & Primal)
*   **Tone:** Ethereal, raspy, eccentric, growling or melodic.
*   **Pacing:** Unpredictable, changing volume.
*   **NPCs:** Willow, Makaria, Kira, Flow.
