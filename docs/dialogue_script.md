# NPC Dialogue Script (Voice Archetypes)

This script defines the dialogue for the 5 key situations in Three-Dragon Ante. These will be used to generate the initial text and eventually recorded as .wav files. Each archetype has multiple variations to keep the tavern atmosphere fresh.

- **Start:**
  - "The cards, like the stars, reveal much to those who listen."
  - "Patience, traveler. The dragons have much to teach us today."
  - "Shall we consult the deck? Its wisdom is older than this tavern."
- **Power:**
  - "A moment of clarity in a sea of chance."
  - "The alignment is perfect. See how the cards respond?"
- **Victory:**

  - "The flow was in my favor. Do not despair; even the tide returns."
